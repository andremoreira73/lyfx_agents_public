Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free tier?

Yes. Our public Agents are free to use (see Agents: Terms of Use).

Can I build my own agent?

No. We collaborate closely with you to design sophisticated, reliable workflows tailored precisely to your business. Our technology specializes in handling complex scenarios, going beyond simple "no-code" solutions.

How do you store my data?

Your data is encrypted at rest, deleted upon your request, and never used for model training without your explicit permission.

What models power AgentVerse?

AgentVerse is powered by cutting-edge LLMs such as Gemini 2.5 and OpenAI's o-series, orchestrated seamlessly with LangGraph. For enhanced security and controlled access, we also offer solutions built with local LLMs.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes. All our agents run seamlessly in the cloud (either your private cloud or a major provider) accessible effortlessly from mobile, tablet, or desktop.
